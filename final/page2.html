The Wonderful World of Elsword

All about that magical land the game is based on, Elrios.

The Elsword world consists of a major supercontinent which was split into two. This supercontinent is called Elrios.
Map of Elrios

LURENSIA
The Elrios supercontinent has been divided into two continents, Lurensia being the western one. Due to the El, Lurensia housed a fertile and prosperous environment and at the same time, filled it with the supreme energy of green earth. The villages on this continent are:
Ruben Village
Elder Village
Bethma Village
Altera Village
Feita Village
Velder Village

A map of Lurensia: Map of Lurensia

FLUONE
The Elrios continent exploded whilst in chaos, causing the colossal mountain to sink within the sea. After several months of pandemonium, the Elrios continent was eventually split into two separate continents... From the Northern Velder Kingdom in Lurensia, one can pass through the Velder Marina to the eastern Elrios Continent called Fluone. The villages on this continent are:
Hamel Capital
Sander Village

There is no map for Fluone, but perhaps when more villages are created in the game they may allow us to see the entirety of the continent.

EXTRA INFO

In the North American(NA) server, Rurensia and Pluone have been renamed as Lurensia and Fluone.
In the Korean version of Elsword: The name Pluone seems to have a certain relation to "Pluone Stones" used to protect items from degradation when upgrading items.
